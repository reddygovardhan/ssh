About SAI SIDDHARTHA HOSPITAL

Sai Siddhartha Hospital is 50 Bedded State Govt. Recognized Hospital with OT and ICU facility located in Shapur nagar, IDA Jedimetla, Hyderabad,Telangana State was found in 2016 offers specialities like Pulmonology, Diabetology, Internal Medicine, general Surgery,ENT,Anaethesiology and Critical Care. It has Lung Surgery Unit, Video Bronchoscopy,Rigid Bronchoscopy, Thoracoscopy,Computerised Lung Function Testing,X-Ray and Lab facilities, Advanced Critical Care and Ventilatory management.
